#+TITLE: My overnight oats
#+DATE: 2018-08-24
#+SETUPFILE: ../recipe.setup

* Ingredients
  - 1 banana
  - 1/2 cup of rolled oats
  - 3/4 cup of oat milk
  - Shredded coconut
  - 1 teaspoon of chia seeds
  - 1-2 chopped dates

* Instructions
  1. Mush banana at the base of a glass jar
  2. Add all other ingredients and mix
