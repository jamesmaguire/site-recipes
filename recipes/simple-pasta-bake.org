#+TITLE: Simple pasta bake
#+DATE: 2024-08-05
#+SETUPFILE: recipe.setup

[[https://www.jaroflemons.com/vegan-pasta-bake/#wprm-recipe-container-145252][Original link]]

* Ingredients
- 340 g pasta (of choice)
- 1 Tbsp olive oil
- 1 1/2 cups diced red onion
- 1 Tbsp minced garlic
- 1 medium bell pepper (finely diced)
- 2 medium carrots (finely diced)
- 1 1/2 cups cooked green lentils
- 700 mL jar marinara sauce
- 400 g can diced tomatoes
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1/2 tsp black pepper
- 1/4 cup chopped fresh basil
- 1/2 cup vegan parmesan
- 1 1/2 cup shredded vegan mozzarella

* Instructions
1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil and cook pasta according to package instructions.
2. Preheat the oven to 220\deg{}C.
3. Add the oil to a skillet and heat over medium heat.
4. Add in the diced onion and saute for about 5-7 minutes, until translucent.
5. Add in the garlic, diced pepper and carrots.
6. Saute for an additional 5 or so minutes.
7. Add in the lentils, diced tomatoes, marinara, salt, and pepper.
8. Stir in the cooked pasta, basil, and parmesan. Mix well to combine.
9. Transfer to a large casserole or baking dish.
10. Top with the mozzarella and bake, uncovered, for 20-25 minutes or until the cheese is melted.
11. Enjoy!
