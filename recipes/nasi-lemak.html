Nasi Lemak

Ingredients

Coconut rice

  • 1.5 cups rice
  • 1/2 cup coconut milk
  • 2 cups water
  • Lemongrass stalk
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • Ginger

Toppings

  • Rendang (Wan Long)
  • Sambal sauce
  • Cucumber
  • Roast peanuts
  • Crispy fried shallots

Instructions

  1. Cook rice
  2. Heat rendang
  3. Prepare toppings
  4. Serve up
