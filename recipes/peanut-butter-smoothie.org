#+TITLE: Peanut butter smoothie
#+DATE: 2020-06-10
#+SETUPFILE: recipe.setup

* Ingredients
** Base
  - 1 Banana
  - 1/2 cups oats
  - 1 tbsp peanut butter
  - 1 tsp hemp seed
  - 2 cups oat milk
  - Dash vanilla extract

** Flairs
   - Pea protein
   - Frozen mango
   - Frozen strawberry
   - LSA/flaxseed

* Method
  1. Add to blender
  2. Blend
