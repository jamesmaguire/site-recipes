#+TITLE: Nasi Lemak
#+DATE: 2023-11-25
#+SETUPFILE: recipe.setup

* Ingredients
** Coconut rice
- 1.5 cups rice
- 1/2 cup coconut milk
- 2 cups water
- Lemongrass stalk
- 3 cloves garlic
- Ginger

** Toppings
- Rendang (Wan Long)
- Sambal sauce
- Cucumber
- Roast peanuts
- Crispy fried shallots

* Instructions
1. Cook rice
2. Heat rendang
3. Prepare toppings
4. Serve up
