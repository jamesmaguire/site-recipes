Edamame Rice

Original link (just using sauce recipe)

Ingredients

Rice/topping

  • Jasmine or other preferred rice
  • Edamame (steamed or microwaved)
  • Peanuts

Sauce

  • 2 tbsp light soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp vegetarian stir fry sauce
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 2 garlic cloves, chopped
  • 1 tbsp chopped scallion and coriander (or scallion only)
  • 1/2 tsp salt (taste your sauce and see how much is needed)
  • 1/2 tsp sugar
  • 1/2 tbsp black vinegar

Instructions

  1. Cook rice and edamame, adjust quantity depending on levels of hunger
  2. Mix sauce ingredients in a small container
  3. To serve, pour sauce over rice and top with edamame and peanuts
