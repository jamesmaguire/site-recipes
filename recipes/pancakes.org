#+TITLE: Pancakes
#+DATE: 2021-10-30
#+SETUPFILE: recipe.setup

* Ingredients

** Wet
- 1 cup flour
- 2 tablespoons organic sugar
- 1 tablespoon baking powder
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
** Dry
- 1 cup non-dairy milk (240 mL)
- 1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar
- 1 teaspoon vanilla

* Method

1. In a medium bowl add dry ingredients and stir to combine.
2. Create a well in dry ingredients and add wet ingredients.
3. Whisk until smooth, then rest for 5 minutes.
4. Cook on pan and serve.
