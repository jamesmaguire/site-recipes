Yogurt mince pasta

Ingredients

Tofu mince

  • 2 tbsp neutral oil
  • 1 red onion chopped
  • 2 cups grated tofu
  • 3 tbsp dark soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp ketchup
  • 1 tbsp vegan beef stock powder (optional)
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp chilli flakes
  • 1 tsp garlic powder

Yogurt sauce

  • 1 cup unsweetened vegan yogurt
  • 1 garlic clove mince
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
  • salt to taste

Paprika butter

  • 4 tbsp vegan butter
  • 1/2 tbsp smoked paprika

Garnish

  • chopped tomato
  • chopped parsley
  • boiled pasta of your choice

Instructions

  1. Cook tofu ingredients low and slow for 25-30 minutes until it dries up and crisps up slightly
  2. Cook pasta
  3. Mix yogurt sauce ingredients
  4. Bring butter to slow boil and add paprika
  5. Top pasta with yogurt, mince then butter
