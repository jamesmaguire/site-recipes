#+TITLE: Salted caramel smoothie
#+DATE: 2022-09-23
#+SETUPFILE: recipe.setup

Inspired by Bare Wholefoods, Sydney

* Ingredients
  - Coconut water
  - Dates (3 per serve)
  - Banana
  - Cinnamon
  - Almond butter (or sub peanut butter)
  - Sea salt

* Instructions
  1. Combine ingredients in preferred quantities in blender and blend
