#+TITLE: Roast vege and quinoa salad
#+DATE: 2024-02-04
#+SETUPFILE: recipe.setup

* Ingredients
- Cooked quinoa (1 cup to 2 cups water)
** Vegies
- Potato
- Sweet potato
- Carrot
- Kale
- Brocolli
** Peanut, ginger and lime sauce
- 1 Clove garlic
- 1 tsp grated ginger
- 3-4 tbsp water
- 6 tbsp natural peanut butter
- 2 tbsp fresh lime juice
- 2 tbsp grapeseed oil
- 1 tbsp soy sauce
- 2 tsp maple syrup
- 1 tsp sriracha
- 1/4 tsp salt

* Instructions
  1. Roast the veggies
  2. Cook the quinoa
  3. Mix the sauce ingredients (either in blender or in a bowl)
  4. Combine and serve
